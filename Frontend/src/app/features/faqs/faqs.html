Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about Travel Services LLC.

1. Is Travel Services LLC a registered company?

Yes, Travel Services LLC is a fully registered entity in the United States under Employer Identification Number (EIN) 30-1453080, with its corporate office in California, USA, and a dedicated customer support and operations office in New Delhi, India.

2. How will I receive my flight tickets or booking confirmations?

Since we provide 100% digital travel management services, all your e-tickets, hotel vouchers, and itineraries will be delivered directly to your registered Email address or WhatsApp number within 24 hours of successful payment.

3. What should I do if I don't receive my e-ticket within 24 hours?

Sometimes, due to technical delays or airline confirmation queues, it might take a little longer. If you do not receive your confirmation within 24 hours, please contact our support team immediately.

4. Can I cancel or change my booking?

Yes, you can request a cancellation or change. Flight bookings, hotel reservations, and tour packages are subject to the specific policies of the respective airline or hotel provider.

5. How long does it take to get a refund after cancellation?

Once the cancellation request is approved by the third-party vendor (airline/hotel), your refund will be processed automatically. The amount will be credited back to your original mode of payment within 5 to 7 working days.

6. Are there any non-refundable fees?

Yes, service fees charged by Travel Services LLC for booking assistance, convenience fees, travel insurance premiums, and certain promotional discounted tickets are non-refundable according to vendor terms.

7. Is my payment secure on your website?

Absolutely. We partner with secure and PCI-compliant payment gateways such as Stripe to process all transactions. Your card and billing details remain encrypted and secure.
