Cancellation & Refund Policy

Transparent cancellation and refund procedures for a hassle-free travel experience.

Policy Overview

At Travel Services LLC, we strive to provide a seamless booking experience. Please read our cancellation and refund policy carefully before booking.

1

Cancellations by the Customer

Flight bookings, hotel reservations, and tour packages are subject to the cancellation policies of the respective airline, hotel, or service provider.

To request a cancellation, customers must contact our support team via email at least 48 hours prior to the scheduled departure or check-in.

2

Refund Process

  • Cancellation request must be approved by the airline/hotel/vendor.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method.
  • Refund processing time is typically 5–7 working days.
3

Non-Refundable Services

  • Travel Services LLC booking assistance fees are non-refundable.
  • Convenience fees are non-refundable.
  • Insurance premiums are non-refundable.
  • Promotional discounted tickets may be non-refundable according to vendor terms.
