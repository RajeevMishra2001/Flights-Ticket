Privacy Policy

At Travel Services LLC, accessible from travelservicellc.netlify.app, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by us and how we use it.

01

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us, including but not limited to your name, email address, phone number, billing address, and passport/travel details when you make a booking or contact support.

02

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our travel services.
  • Process your payments and prevent fraudulent transactions.
  • Send booking confirmations, itinerary updates, and support messages.
  • Comply with legal and tax obligations under US and Indian laws.
03

Log Files and Cookies

Travel Services LLC follows a standard procedure of using log files and cookies. These files log visitors when they visit websites.

The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, and referring/exit pages. This information is used for analyzing trends and administering the site.

04

Data Protection and Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We only share information with trusted third-party service providers such as airlines, hotels, and secure payment gateways when strictly necessary to fulfill your travel bookings.

05

GDPR & CCPA Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location (US/EU), you have the right to request access to, correction of, or erasure of your personal data.
